Solvent Cement

Introducing Krischo Solvent Cement: Bonding Excellence for Your Piping Solutions Welcome to a world of seamless connections and lasting bonds with Krischo Solvent Cement. Engineered for precision and reliability, our solvent cement is the adhesive solution that holds your piping systems together with strength and durability.
1. Superior Bonding Strength: Krischo Solvent Cement is formulated for superior bonding strength. It creates robust connections, ensuring a reliable and long-lasting seal for your piping systems.
2. Quick Setting Formula: Efficiency is paramount. Our solvent cement features a quick-setting formula, minimizing downtime during installation. This rapid bonding process accelerates your project timelines without compromising on quality.
3. Versatile Applications: Krischo Solvent Cement is designed for versatility. Whether you're working with PVC, CPVC, or other plastic piping systems, our solvent cement provides a secure and effective bond for various applications.
