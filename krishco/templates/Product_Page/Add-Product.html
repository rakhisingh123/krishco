SELF PRIMING PUMP

KRISHCO Regenerative Self Priming Pumpsets are made from top-grade castings, finest components and high quality gun metal using state-of-the-art technology, to conform to the latest International Standards. Each & every stage in the manufacturing process is closely monitored through stringent quality tests to ensure impeccable standards, superior performance and unmatched durability. Pump volute chambers and impellers are designed to give the best hydraulic efficiency. These pumpsets are powered by totally enclosed fan cooled two pole motors with stators of the motors are made up of low att loss lamination and dynamically balanced rotors for higher efficiency. These pumpsets are wound with high grade enameled copper wire and are fitted with thermal overload protector.
